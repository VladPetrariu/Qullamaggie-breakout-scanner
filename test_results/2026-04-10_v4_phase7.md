## Winsorization Impact

Winsorization mostly confirms the raw numbers — outliers aren't as distortionary as expected at 5d (raw avg -0.30% vs W.Avg -0.33%). The bigger impact is at 10d (raw -0.48% vs W.Avg -0.43%), where some extreme multi-day swings get clipped.

## Key Takeaways

1. **Ranking tweaks were neutral.** ATR floor, volume ratio, and HH/HL weighting didn't move headline metrics. The same stocks pass the quality gate; only the ordering changed slightly.
2. **SPY benchmark adds context.** 51% win rate means more when the market is down. Picks beat SPY 52.2% of the time — modest but real alpha vs the index.
3. **Excess median is positive.** +0.20% median excess over SPY at 5d. Average is negative because a few big losers drag it down (tail risk).
4. **Top 5 problem persists.** ATR floor didn't fix it. May require a position-diversity constraint (don't put 3 similar stocks in top 5) or a cap on rank correlation.
5. **Phase 8 multi-factor combos are the next lever.** Individual factor tweaks are hitting diminishing returns within this quality-gated pool. The next improvement likely comes from finding which factor *combinations* produce the highest win rates.
